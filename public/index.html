A short intro about us

Sky Blazers is an international sky, trail and ultrarunning team with pro and amateur runners. It traces its origins back in 2015 when three friends decided to participate in the Tromsø Sky Race of that same year. Ever since, this group of friends have added more thrill seekers willing to explore the limits of the body and mind in jaw dropping landscapes. We are not “just” a bunch of runners, the Sky Blazers want to connect more people with nature, sharing the beautiful outdoors and inspiring people to go outside and explore those places.
