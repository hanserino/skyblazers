About

A short intro about us

Sky Blazers is an international sky, trail and ultrarunning team with pro and amateur runners. It traces its origins back in 2015 when three friends (Felipe, Hans Kristian and Johannes), decided to participate in the Tromsø Sky Race of that same year. Ever since, this group of friends have grown to have more and more people of all ages and nationalities willing to explore the limits of the body and mind in amazing landscapes.

Oslo, our backyard

We ran and train in all the surrounding woodlands and trails of Oslo Marka.

We also organise runs every Tuesdays, the famously known Stirsdag — check it out.
