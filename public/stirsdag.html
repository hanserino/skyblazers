Stirsdag

WTF is Stirsdag?

Every Tuesday, Strava feeds from all over the world get flooded by people who were out and running in woods and on trails. They also look amazingly happy too. Their activity is usually called Stirsdag. What’s up with that?

Now you going to find out…

Stirsdag — or Trail Tuesdays —, is the best day of the week. A gang of people meet at Disen tram stop and run together on trails of Lillomarka.

When does it happen?

Every single freakin’ Tuesday at 18:00. All year’ound.

Who’s invited?

Anyone!

How long is the course?

Around 12 km + 550 m elevation gain.
