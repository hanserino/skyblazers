Films

Besatt

Besatt is a 25 minute long documentary film about Johannes Rummelhoff's FKT attempt the summer of 2018 where he ran from Oslo to Galdhøpiggen - a 401km long trail in 4 days, 8 hours, 39 minutes and 44 seconds. The film covers the journey from Oslo to Galdhøpiggen as well as the friendship in the Sky Blazers crew which helped him achieve this monstrous effort.
