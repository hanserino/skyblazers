The Blazin’ Podcast

The Blazin’ Podcast is a spin off of Hans Kristian’s podcast NÅ ER DET ALVOR. Since he’s out traveling these days he decided to create another podcast which is 100% English. It will mostly be about running, mountaineering and outdoors stuff, but also interviews with random people he meets on his journey.

Why call it "The Blazin’ Podcast"? ’Cause we’re The Sky Blazers and whe blaze the sky, mountains and trails. That’s why.
